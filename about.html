Welcome to Meswo Medical

We are here to ensure you are healthy

Located in Kapsabet, Meswo Medical Hospital is a modern hospital facility which is home to highly skilled and experienced specialists and professional medical staff. For generations, we have been ranked among the best private hospitals in Kenya because of our excellent standards and the high quality of services and personal care we provide. We are pacesetters in our field, inspired by our core value of care. We care about the dignity of our patients and strive to become a one-stop healthcare provider in Kenya. The scope of the social service league includes the main hospital in Kisumu and the Kapsabet Branch.
